Welcome To Via Margutta Gallery

A Brief history of the Ecuadorian Painter, Artist and Educator, Luis Miranda Neira. Along with an exhibition of his private collection held in the art gallery located in Guayaquil, Ecuador named Via Margutta Gallery.

"I painted hippies, homeless people, jazz artists from latin Harlem. I've always cherished and connected with the lower and middle class. Never tried to paint the people of high class, I always look for the townspeople because I connect with them."

- Luis Miranda Neira
