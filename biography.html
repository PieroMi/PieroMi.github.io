Biography


Born April 15, 1932, Guayaquil, Ecuador

Graduated Vicente Rocafuerte High School, 1950

Graduated College from Escuela de Bellas Artes de Guayaquil, 1955

Graduated from The School of Arts in Rome, 1961

Awards


Via Margutta Award, 1959

Premio Fiat, 1959

Vittorio Grassi, 1960

Copa Odescalchi, 1960

Silver Medal for Il Giornale Di Italy, 1960

Salon De Julio & Salon De Octubre, 1973

Primer Premio de Pintura, 1976

Premio Nacional de Pintura, Banco Central, 1978

Pintura Armada Nacional del Ecuador, 1982

Gold Medal "Al Mérito Artístico”, 1984

Nuestro Mar de la Marina del Ecuador, 1987
